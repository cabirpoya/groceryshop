Set Up Your DeepSite Account** - Go to [DeepSite](https://deepsite.io/) (or their official website). - Sign up for an account (free or paid plan, depending on your needs).  ---  ### **2. Create a New Project** - Click **"New Project"**. - Choose **"Web App"** (since you need user roles and database functionality). - Name it (e.g., **"Grocery Shop Management"**).  ---  ### **3. Define Your Database Structure** DeepSite allows you to create tables (like Excel sheets) to store data.  #### **Tables You Need:** 1. **Users Table** (for Admins & Users)    - Fields: `UserID`, `Name`, `Email`, `Password`, `Role` (Admin/User), `AssignedCategories` (optional).    2. **Products Table** (for Inventory)    - Fields: `ProductID`, `Name`, `Category` (Electronics, Medicine, Food, etc.), `Price`, `Stock`, `Supplier`.  3. **Sales Table** (for Transactions)    - Fields: `SaleID`, `ProductID`, `Quantity`, `Date`, `SoldBy` (UserID).  4. **Categories Table** (Your 8 Items)    - Fields: `CategoryID`, `Name`.  ---  ### **4. Set Up User Roles & Permissions** - Go to **"User Management"** in DeepSite. - Create **2 Admin accounts** (full access). - Create **8 User accounts** (restricted access). - Assign **permissions**:   - **Admins** can:     - View all products.     - Add/edit users.     - Generate reports (balance sheet, inventory).   - **Users** can:     - Only see their assigned categories.     - Add sales for their items.     - Check stock levels (but not modify them).  ---  ### **5. Design the Admin Dashboard** - Use DeepSite’s **drag-and-drop editor** to create:   - **Dashboard View** (showing total sales, stock alerts).   - **User Management** (add/edit users).   - **Inventory Overview** (all products).   - **Reports Section** (balance sheet, sales analytics).  ---  ### **6. Design the User Dashboard** - Each user sees only their assigned categories (e.g., "Medicine" or "Electronics"). - Features:   - **Sell Products** (select item, enter quantity).   - **Check Stock** (only for their category).   - **View Sales History** (only their transactions).  ---  ### **7. Set Up Balance Sheet & Inventory Reports** - Use DeepSite’s **"Reports"** feature to create:   - **Daily Sales Report** (total revenue).   - **Stock Alert** (low inventory warnings).   - **Profit/Loss Statement** (income vs. expenses).  ---  ### **8. Test & Deploy** - **Test Admin & User Logins**:   - Ensure admins see everything.   - Ensure users see only their categories. - **Check Sales & Inventory Updates**:   - Verify stock decreases after a sale.   - Confirm balance sheet updates correctly. - **Publish Your App**:   - Deploy on DeepSite’s hosting or export if needed.  ---  ### **Final Features Checklist** ✅ **2 Admins** (full control)   ✅ **8 Users** (restricted to their categories)   ✅ **8 Product Categories** (Electronics, Medicine, Food, etc.)   ✅ **Inventory & Stock Management**   ✅ **Sales Tracking**   ✅ **Balance Sheet & Reports**    ---  ### **Alternative: Use DeepSite + Google Sheets (If Easier)** - If DeepSite’s database is complex, you can:   1. Store product data in **Google Sheets**.   2. Connect it to DeepSite using **API/Zapier**.   3. Use **DeepSite for the frontend** (user roles, UI).  ---
